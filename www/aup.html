Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This policy describes the rules for using the whoswhere service. By accessing whoswhere, you agree to follow these rules. Levenick Enterprises (“we,” “us”) may update this policy from time to time; the “Last updated” date above reflects the most recent revision.

1. Who this applies to

This policy applies to every user who signs into a whoswhere tenant — admins, audit users, and anyone else with access to the application.

2. Acceptable uses

whoswhere is built for managing construction crews and project assignments. Acceptable use includes coordinating which crew members are at which jobsites, maintaining accurate records of project and personnel data, and using the application’s reporting and assignment features in the ordinary course of your business.

3. Prohibited uses

You may not use whoswhere to:

  • Engage in illegal activity, or store data whose collection violates the law.
  • Harass, discriminate against, or store hostile content about the people whose records are in the system.
  • Run automated scrapers, bulk-request floods, or any other traffic that interferes with normal application use.
  • Test for security vulnerabilities without our prior written consent. We’re happy to coordinate responsible-disclosure conversations; just email first.
  • Share, sell, or transfer your sign-in credentials to anyone outside your organization.
  • Use the service in a way that puts disproportionate load on the infrastructure beyond what reasonable construction-crew sizes require.
  • Attempt to access another tenant’s data or otherwise circumvent the tenant-isolation boundaries that whoswhere enforces.

4. Enforcement

If we believe you’ve violated this policy, we may suspend or terminate your access to whoswhere. For serious violations (illegal activity, security threats, abuse) we may act immediately and without prior notice. For less serious matters we’ll typically notify you and offer a chance to correct the issue.

5. Reporting violations

If you observe a violation of this policy — by another user of your tenant, by a third party, or by us — please report it to hello@whos-where.com.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@whos-where.com.
