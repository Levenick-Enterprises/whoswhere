whoswhere

A digital magnet board for construction crews. Drag, drop, done.

Foremen and superintendents track which crew member is at which jobsite the same way they’ve always done it — a board on the trailer wall, magnets shuffled at the start of the day. We made that board work on a phone.

What it does

  • Mobile-first. Built for the foreman’s phone on a jobsite, not a desktop in an office. Works fine on a tablet or computer too.
  • Drag-and-drop reassignments. Move a person between projects with a touch. Real-time across every device looking at the board — the office sees the change immediately.
  • Your own URL, your own database. Each customer gets a private subdomain and an isolated database. Your crew data doesn’t share a server with anyone else’s.
