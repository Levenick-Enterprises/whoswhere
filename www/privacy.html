Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This policy describes how Levenick Enterprises (“we,” “us”) collects and uses information when you access the whoswhere service.

1. Who we are

whoswhere is operated by Levenick Enterprises. We provide a private, per-tenant application that helps construction foremen and superintendents track which crew members are at which projects.

2. Information we collect

Account information. Your email address, used to authenticate you via a one-time numeric sign-in code. We don’t store passwords; we don’t use a marketing list.

Operational data. Whatever your tenant’s authorized users put into the application: project names and metadata (numbers, addresses, role assignments), crew member names and metadata (employee numbers, positions, phone numbers, notes), and the assignments between them. The tenant decides what data goes in.

Server logs. Standard request metadata (IP address, user agent, timestamp, route) retained briefly for debugging and abuse prevention.

3. How we use the information

We use the data above to operate the service: render the application, persist your edits, authenticate you, and respond to support requests. We don’t sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

4. How we store the information

Each tenant’s data lives in its own dedicated Postgres database, isolated from every other tenant by both database boundary and row-level security policies. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Sessions are maintained via short-lived HTTP-only cookies.

5. Service providers (subprocessors)

We rely on a small set of vendors to operate the service:

  • Supabase — database hosting and authentication infrastructure.
  • Vercel — application hosting and edge networking.
  • Resend — sending one-time sign-in code emails.
  • Cloudflare — DNS and email forwarding.

Each of these vendors has its own privacy policy; we link them above. We will update this list if we add or change a subprocessor.

6. Retention

We retain operational data while your account is active. If you stop using the service or close the account, you can request deletion of all data associated with your tenant via the contact email below. Server logs roll off on a short cycle (weeks, not years).

7. Children’s privacy

whoswhere is intended for use by businesses and is not directed at children. We don’t knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16. If you believe we’ve done so, contact us and we’ll delete it.

8. Cookies and similar technologies

We use only the essential authentication cookies set by Supabase Auth so that signing in works. We don’t use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party trackers.

9. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the data we hold about you by emailing the contact below. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under local data protection law (for example, the GDPR in the EU or the CCPA in California). Tell us which rights you’d like to exercise and we’ll respond.

10. Changes to this policy

When we make material changes to this policy, we’ll update the “Last updated” date at the top and, for changes that affect how we handle your data, notify tenants directly. Continued use of the service after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy question or request, email hello@whos-where.com.
